About the Project

This is a platform hosting itineraries for three geo-archaeo hiking trails, with each trail telling part of the island’s history, linking unique geological areas and known archaeological sites.

Exploring the Islands

Don't just visit the islands - walk your way through their rich history and unique geological formations and understand them.

Our Impact

By creating narratives of landscapes and heritage sites with the communities in the archipelago, the project aims to influence the way we engage with our surroundings and to gain an understanding of the direct impact that our actions have - that while our visits are temporary, our actions on the environment and in the landscape can be permanent.
